POP planning and organisatie - 2024

Task description:

The task is to organize my weekly schedule, making sure that I balance lessons, personal time, studying, and other activities. I have tracked my time for a week to ensure efficient management of my daily tasks.

Personal challenges:

A major challenge was creating a schedule that included all my necessary tasks while also ensuring I have enough time for rest and leisure. It required me to balance academic responsibilities (38 hours of class time) with other activities such as personal study time, meals, and family or hobby time. Finding the right balance took a lot of trial and error and thoughtful planning.

What I have learned:

It taught me how to prioritize tasks and allocate time wisely. I now understand better how to avoid overloading myself and ensure there is enough time for both work and relaxation. This exercise improved my ability to organize my week and helped me focus on what’s most important at the right times.

What I have learned:

This project improved my skills in C# and XAML and taught me how to solve problems that arose during the development process.
