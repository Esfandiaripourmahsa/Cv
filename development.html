Development

Why Programming?

- Why the education programming?

I am a student and I work hard to obtain my diploma. IT has always interested me because I enjoy working with technology. I spend a lot of time on my laptop or smartphone, and the idea of creating such technology myself is very motivating. I am creative and love designing, especially websites, and I enjoy bringing my ideas to life.

Additionally, I want to continue developing myself both personally and professionally. This diploma is a big first step into the IT world. In the future, I want to earn more diplomas and certifications. Friends working in IT have encouraged me, and now I see how valuable it is to grow in this field.

- Why is this profession right for me?

I love IT and want to work in this field. Programming combines creativity, logical thinking, and technology, which aligns well with my interests. It gives me the opportunity to build new things and find solutions to real problems.

I work hard to achieve my goals and I want to turn my passion for technology into a career.
